Our History

Established in 2016 and only had one branch in Kubang Kerian, Kelantan at the beginning.

It has more than 30 branches in Malaysia and offers a variety of coffee menus such as black

coffee, latte coffee, to coffee, milk espresso coffee Apart from coffee, it also offers a variety

of other food and drinks, such as baked bread, fried bananas, and fruit juice.
